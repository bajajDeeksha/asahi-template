Who we are

We are Asahi Service Company, UR agents. UR stands for Urban Renaissance and is an independent administrative corporation formed by the Japanese national government.All you need to move in to a UR housing apartment is advance Rent + Management Fee of the first month plus deposit. Deposit are 2 months rent and 1-2 months for campaign properties. This makes it affordable and easy to have the property you want. Our English speaking consultants can organize you to view inside apartments today!

What we do

We help foreigners in Japan to get apartment according to their budget and provide our services to improve living environment and provides Comprehensive Support for Foreign Nationals. We are actively assisting our clients through out the process of getting apartment for lifestyle support for everyone like foreign nationals and anyone who has a desire to rent an apartmrnt so that everyone can start their new lives in Japan smoothly.
